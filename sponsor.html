Overview

The Second Annual Budstock Music Festival is a vibrant celebration of music, culture, and community, set to take place at Electric Echo Lake. This year's festival promises an exciting lineup of music concerts and a Harvest Fest, creating a unique and immersive experience for all attendees.

Artists can apply to perform and can leverage Budstock to release their music and merchandise to the cannabis community at Budstock. This opportunity is open to all artists, and we especially encourage emerging talent from New York to apply before July 30, 2024.
