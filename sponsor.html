Media & Highlights

Larger Scale Festivals

Budstock has grown from intimate gatherings to a full-fledged "harvest fest for peace, love, music, and ganja," drawing in large crowds.

Legal Cannabis Sales

In 2023, Budstock made history as the first music festival in New York to legally sell cannabis on-site, setting a new standard.

Multi-Day Immersive Experience

Attendees can now enjoy Budstock over three days and two nights, with camping options for a truly immersive experience.

Cannabis Growers Showcase

Licensed New York cultivators join Budstock to educate attendees about their products, fostering a greater understanding of the industry.

Curated Bud Bar

Experience a curated selection of cannabis offerings from various cultivators at the festival's Bud Bar.

Diverse Entertainment

From live music performances by top artists like Jadakiss to appearances by celebrities from shows like Love and Hip Hop, Budstock offers a wide array of entertainment.
