About Ganja Pigs International

Born in the heart of New York, Ganja Pigs started as a grassroots movement, hosting intimate gatherings for cannabis enthusiasts. Under the visionary leadership of Jonathan Rader, we've evolved into a cultural phenomenon, orchestrating large-scale events that celebrate the intersection of cannabis, music, and community.
